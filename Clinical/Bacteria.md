| **Bacteria**                      | **Gram Stain** | **Respiration**           | **Primary Infection**    | **Primary Treatment Options**                                |
| --------------------------------- | -------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **_Staphylococcus aureus_**       | Positive       | Facultative Anaerobe      | Skin infections, Sepsis  | **Nafcillin/Oxacillin** (MSSA); **Vancomycin** (MRSA)        |
| **_Streptococcus pyogenes_**      | Positive       | Facultative Anaerobe      | Strep throat, Cellulitis | **Penicillin G/V** or **Amoxicillin**                        |
| **_Streptococcus pneumoniae_**    | Positive       | Facultative Anaerobe      | Pneumonia, Meningitis    | **Ceftriaxone**, **Amoxicillin**, or **Levofloxacin**        |
| **_Enterococcus faecalis_**       | Positive       | Facultative Anaerobe      | UTIs, Endocarditis       | **Ampicillin** (often + Gentamicin)                          |
| **_Bacillus anthracis_**          | Positive       | Obligate Aerobe           | Anthrax                  | **Ciprofloxacin** or **Doxycycline**                         |
| **_Clostridium difficile_**       | Positive       | **Obligate Anaerobe**     | Colitis (GI tract)       | **Oral Vancomycin** or **Fidaxomicin**                       |
| **_Clostridium tetani_**          | Positive       | **Obligate Anaerobe**     | Tetanus                  | **Metronidazole** (plus Tetanus Ig)                          |
| **_Listeria monocytogenes_**      | Positive       | Facultative Anaerobe      | Listeriosis (GI/CNS)     | **Ampicillin**                                               |
| **_Corynebacterium diphtheriae_** | Positive       | Facultative Anaerobe      | Diphtheria (Throat)      | **Erythromycin** or **Penicillin**                           |
| **_Propionibacterium acnes_**     | Positive       | **Aerotolerant Anaerobe** | Acne (Skin)              | **Benzoyl Peroxide**, **Clindamycin**, **Doxycycline**       |
| **_Escherichia coli_**            | Negative       | Facultative Anaerobe      | UTIs, GI upset           | **Nitrofurantoin**, **TMP-SMX** (Bactrim), **Ciprofloxacin** |
| **_Klebsiella pneumoniae_**       | Negative       | Facultative Anaerobe      | Pneumonia, UTIs          | **Ceftriaxone** or **Carbapenems** (if ESBL)                 |
| **_Salmonella enterica_**         | Negative       | Facultative Anaerobe      | Typhoid, Food poisoning  | **Ciprofloxacin** or **Ceftriaxone**                         |
| **_Pseudomonas aeruginosa_**      | Negative       | **Obligate Aerobe**       | Burn/Ventilator PNA      | **Piperacillin-Tazobactam** (Zosyn), **Cefepime**            |
| **_Neisseria meningitidis_**      | Negative       | Obligate Aerobe           | Meningitis (CNS)         | **Ceftriaxone** or **Penicillin G**                          |
| **_Neisseria gonorrhoeae_**       | Negative       | Obligate Aerobe           | Gonorrhea (STD)          | **Ceftriaxone** (IM)                                         |
| **_Haemophilus influenzae_**      | Negative       | Facultative Anaerobe      | Epiglottitis, Ear inf.   | **Amoxicillin-Clavulanate** (Augmentin)                      |
| **_Helicobacter pylori_**         | Negative       | **Microaerophilic**       | Stomach ulcers           | **Clarithromycin** + **Amoxicillin** + PPI                   |
| **_Campylobacter jejuni_**        | Negative       | **Microaerophilic**       | Gastroenteritis          | **Azithromycin**                                             |
| **_Vibrio cholerae_**             | Negative       | Facultative Anaerobe      | Cholera (GI tract)       | **Doxycycline** or **Azithromycin**                          |
