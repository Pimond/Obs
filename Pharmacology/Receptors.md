| **Receptor** | **G-Protein** | **Primary Distribution **                                    | **Cellular/Physiological Actions **                                      |
| ------------ | ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **$M_1$**    | $G_q$         | CNS, Autonomic ganglia, Gastric glands                       | ↑ CNS excitation, ↑ Gastric acid secretion                               |
| **$M_2$**    | $G_i$         | **Heart (Atria, SA/AV nodes)**, Presynaptic terminals        | ↓ Heart rate (Bradycardia), ↓ Conduction velocity                        |
| **$M_3$**    | $G_q$         | **Exocrine glands**, **Smooth muscle**, Vascular endothelium | ↑ Secretions (saliva, sweat), Bronchoconstriction, Vasodilation (via NO) |
| **$M_4$**    | $G_i$         | CNS (Striatum)                                               | Inhibition of transmitter release, Locomotor regulation                  |
| **$M_5$**    | $G_q$         | CNS (Substantia nigra), Salivary glands                      | Dopamine release regulation, Vasodilation of cerebral arteries           |

- Odd numbered: $G_{q}$
- Even numbered: $G_i$ 


| **Receptor**   | **G-Protein** | **Signaling Pathway**                                                           | **Result**                          |
| -------------- | ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| **$\alpha_1$** | **$G_q$**     | Activates **Phospholipase C (PLC)** $\rightarrow$ increases **$IP_3$** and DAG. | Vasoconstriction, pupil dilation.   |
| **$\alpha_2$** | **$G_i$**     | Inhibits Adenylyl Cyclase $\rightarrow$ decreases **cAMP**.                     | Inhibits NE release.                |
| **$\beta_1$**  | **$G_s$**     | Activates Adenylyl Cyclase $\rightarrow$ increases **cAMP**.                    | Increased heart rate/contractility. |
| **$\beta_2$**  | **$G_s$**     | Activates Adenylyl Cyclase $\rightarrow$ increases **cAMP**.                    | Bronchodilation, vasodilation.      |
